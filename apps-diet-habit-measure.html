Anatomy of eating behavior:

The counting methods are as follows: Questions 1 to 7, 3 points for "always", 2 points for "often", 1 point for "occasionally", and 0 points for "rarely".

For question numbers 8-12, answer "always" with 0 points, "often" with 1 point, "occasionally" with 2 points, and "rarely" with 3 points.

Eating behavior scoring instructions: 0-12 points, miserable! Crap! Your eating behavior is very bad, it is recommended to make up your mind to lose weight.

13-20 points, although you are a little better than 0-12 points, don't "laugh like a turtle without a tail", please try to change your eating habits.

21-30 points, come on! come on! It will be even better if the eating habits continue to improve the small deficiencies.

31-36 points, you are awesome! Please persevere, you can easily get healthy!
